Therynn

God of gray

History

Therynn has historically been confusing to evreyone else. They do whatever they feel like without adhering to any kind of consistency. One time Therynn doused someone in gray paint for being rude in Therynns temple, another Therynn simply dyed half of the persons clothing permanently in differing shades of grey all somehow not matching the other pieces. Therynn is also known for once accidentally destroying a whole town by claiming that everyone in the vicinity is the villain to someone and therefore shouldn't worry about doing right or wrong, so there's always going to be someone who views you negatively. This then led to a group who goes around eradicating what they view as evil hearing about it and thinking that a god said that the whole village is evil. After hearing about it Therynn just said oops and afterward only said so to highly important followers or people in towns and cities that Therynn felt like they were offended by while in a bad mood. Therynn has also on several occasions helped out while being in a good mood. An example is when a town had a town hall that had a roof that was leaking whitout a clear cause for the leakage. To fix it, Therynn coated the whole roof in grey paint that was water resistant that was easy to remove when the villagers wanted to do repairs on the roof.

Social

Therynn has been known for being one of the more active gods that are not viewed as evil or good. One of the things that Therynn does actively is messing with painters and others who claim to know all shades of gray. Not because Therynn views it as heretical or disrespectful, but because they think it's a great honor for someone to deticate that much time to learning grays. What Therynn does to mess with those people is that Therynn or one of their angels go to that person and asks to name 5 shades of gray, however they are new variants that have not been seen before. The reactions of those people when it happens is something that Therynn finds to be incredibly funny. Therynn is mostly prayed to by those who think that the standards of most gods that are viewed as good are absurd.Therynn is a god who thinks that evil and good are just what people claim to be right or wrong, but why care about that when you can do what you feel like is the best deciscion in that moment? It is due to that kind of thinking that Therynn differntiates between what gender they are at any given moment. Due to Therynn being god over both gray as a color and morally gray, most rituals in Therynns name are done with gray color due to the more physical nature of the color. The only way to know Therynns current mood is by observing what they do in the current moment.

Phethys

God of death and longing

History

Phethys was once viewed as both the god of death and the god of longing, but that has since long stopped being the case. Phethys has never lost the divinity of death. But society has for centuries thought Phethys and the god of death are two separate gods, one that you pray to when you're longing for something, and one that no one wants to get in contact with. Phethys once had a profecy revealed. It states that Phethys would be doing her job of making sure that the soul properly leaves the living so that it can wander freely to whichever afterlife it has destined, Phethys will find the companion that Phethys seeks to have as company in Phethys' divine realm. This causes Phethys to have been seen by many who have been on the brink of death several times. Those people are those who Phethys has found interest in and has been observing in their near death state to see if they are the one that Phethys is looking for. However these sightings have often been seen as an omen of disasters. But the disasters are often what cause the increase in near-death activies of people like adventurers that causes Phethys to be interested. This caused a fear of the god of death, which is why Phethys was the one who made it so that people view Phethys as just the god of longing. Phethys was one of the few gods who were already keeping balance and therefore Jeffrey the immortal god of orcas didn't have to intervene, which caused her to be one of the few gods that didn't participate when the other gods attacked Jeffrey.

Social

Phethys is mostly prayed to by people temporarily, during a part of their life where the devotees are feeling a strong longing for something. These people usually leave after a few months, after their longing has been relieved enough to not be a bother or have manage to purge their longing entirely. Phethys rarely needs to personally step in due to the priests and other such following of Phethys being skilled at helping people in such mindsets. This has caused them to be seen as something akin to a therapist. They are however just clergy who can handle most of the work, but Phethys sometimes also helps them in their tasks. There are also cases when Phethys has to personally step in. When it comes to Phethys' dominion of death, it's mainly people who don't want to die or people who wishes for the death of others. These prayers are mostly ingored due to Phethys only wanting to make sure that the soul leaves the body when it's time, not deciding when the time has come for death. Phethys wouldn't be able to deal with making all souls move on if not thanks to the ability to make the consiousness appear at several places at once. Most gods would solve such issues by sending something like an angel, but Phethys doesn't need to and doesn't have enough divine envoys to do that. Phethys knows the pain of longing and thus does a lot to make others not experience the same thing.
